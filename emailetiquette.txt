DO’s (Follow These)
Use a clear subject line (mention purpose)
Start with a formal greeting (Dear Professor / Sir)
Keep email short and to the point
Use proper grammar and punctuation
Always include a professional closing and signature

DON’Ts (Avoid These)
Don’t use slang or informal words (like “hey”, “lol”)
Don’t write very long emails
Don’t use all caps or too many exclamation marks
Don’t send emails without subject line
Don’t make demands—always be polite
