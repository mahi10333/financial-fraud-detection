# 📱 Responsible Social Media Use Checklist

## ✅ Do's
- Think before you post anything online  
- Use strong privacy settings on your accounts  
- Share accurate and verified information  
- Be respectful and polite in comments and messages  
- Maintain a professional profile and digital image  

## ❌ Don'ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly  
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content  
- Do not spread fake news or unverified information  
- Avoid engaging in online arguments or cyberbullying  
- Do not misuse someone else's content without permission
