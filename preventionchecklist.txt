# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## 🔐 General Safety Tips
- Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or passwords with anyone
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Always verify the identity of the person before making payments
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions

## 💳 UPI / Financial Safety (Important)
- Do not approve “collect requests” without verifying the sender
- Remember: You do NOT need to enter PIN to receive money
- Check payment details carefully before confirming transactions
- Use only official banking and payment apps

## 🚨 Awareness & Reporting
- Regularly monitor your bank statements for unusual activity
- Immediately report fraud to your bank
- Report cybercrime at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930

## 🎓 For Students
- Be cautious of fake internship or job offers asking for money
- Do not share personal details on unknown websites
