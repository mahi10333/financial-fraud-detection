# 🔐 Stay Safe Online – Checklist (For College Students in India)

## ✅ Prevention Tips
- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone  
- Always verify payment requests before approving (UPI apps show “Pay” vs “Receive”)  
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links received via SMS, email, or WhatsApp  
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or financial transactions  
- Download apps only from official sources like Play Store or App Store  
- Check website URLs carefully before entering sensitive information  
- Do not share personal details (Aadhaar, phone number, address) publicly on social media  
- Keep your devices updated with latest security patches  

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930
